OUR STORY

JNANANANDA EDUCATIONAL SOCIETY

The Society has been registered under the Societies Registration Act XXI of 1860 and is catering to the educational needs of the pupils aged 3-18 (Pre-Primary to Senior Intermediate) for over 3 decades.

The Society has constituted a Managing Committee whose board members are dedicated to promoting educational standards in villages of West Godavari and Krishna districts.

We strongly believe in our motto of “Knowledge is Power” and that education should not to be confined to the four walls of the classroom. Hence, every student is educated, empowered, and enriched with the set of five core values: Respect, Commitment, Integrity, Compassion and Perseverance.


Curriculum

Our schools are affiliated to SSC Board, A.P and the Intermediate college is affiliated to Board of Intermediate Education, A.P. The Junior college students pursue MPC, BiPC or CEC streams in English and Telugu medium

Since the first batch in 2007-08, our 10th Class students are the toppers in the SSC board exams at the Mandal level. We proudly state that we have won the distinction of securing 100% pass percentage till date

We at Jnanananda Academy are inspired by the desire to synthesize the best of modern technology in education with core human values so that our students improve their learning outcomes.

For over three decades, we have sustained the momentum of keeping ourselves in the top bracket of ‘Quality Teaching’ and we promise to continue to strive further to better the best performance, prepare the students for the challenges beyond the classroom and provide an ideal education to ensure that every child gets a fair exposure to academics, sports and cultural activities for the inculcation of qualities like co-operation, team-work, tolerance and consideration towards others and thus prepare them for the next Century.

To make the dream of English Medium education a reality for the rural students, Shri. Datla China Rama Chandra Raju established Jnanananda Public School Educational Society in the year 1989. The Society endeavors to provide the best quality education to the children in the rural areas of West Godavari and Krishna districts of Andhra Pradesh

The Society has been registered under the Societies Registration Act XXI of 1860 and is catering to the educational needs of the pupils aged 3-18 (Pre-Primary to Senior Intermediate) for over 3 decades.

The Society is purely service oriented. It runs its schools with a nominal fee structure to ensure that education is within the reach of the poor students. The Society is self-financing and works with its own resources. The schools and college are unaided and do not receive any aid from either the State government or the Central government.

Education at Jnanananda Academy has always been a blend of modern educational pedagogy with traditional values. With consistent hard work and commitment, the Institution has grown steadily over the years. It has started as a pre-primary school in 1989 and is now meeting the educational needs till Senior Intermediate level.

  • D.Ch. R.R. Junior College (English & Telugu Medium)- Elurupadu.
  • Jnanananda English Medium Primary School- Kalla
  • Jnanananda Play School- Doddanapudu

Beyond the classroom

Our approach is to recognize that each child is different and can offer something new to the world.

Campus

The school's beautiful 2 acres campus provides ample green space and fresh air. We have spacious classrooms and playground.

Laboratories

The school and college have Biology, Physics, Chemistry, and a Computer Lab

Transport

We have 5 buses to commute students from 25 villages across West Godavari and Krishna Districts.

Extra-Curricular Activities

Apart from regular academics, students participate in several outdoor activities and sports like Kho-Kho, Volleyball, Throw ball etc.

Academic Progress

Jnanananda Public School has been recognized as one of the best schools in West Godavari District, Andhra Pradesh. Our students have received several prestigious Academic Achievement like the Inspire Awards and Pratibha Awards from the Education Department


Even during Corona pandemic our students could achieve a clear 10/10 GPA. Over the years thousands of students have passed out of Jnanananda Academy. Many of them are in high profile jobs making a positive contribution to the society putting into practice the ethical, human and moral values they imbibed during the course of their education at Jnanananda Academy.
